About me

Myke Morais de Oliveira is a Doctoral candidate in the Computer Science and Computational Mathematics program at the University of São Paulo. He has a Master's degree in Computer Engineering from the Federal University of Rio Grande and a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from the Cruz Alta University. He is a member of the Applied Computing in Education Lab (CAEd). Currently, his main research topics are Machine Learning, Data Science, and Educational Data Mining.
